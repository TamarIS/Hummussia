Our Story

Rooted in tradition · Grown in Berlin

From the Levant to Prenzlauer Berg

die Hummussia was born in 2018 from a simple belief: that the best food is honest food. Hummus — ground chickpeas, tahini, lemon and garlic — has nourished people across the Middle East for centuries. We bring that tradition to Berlin, unchanged and uncompromised.

Every plate starts with chickpeas soaked overnight and cooked until perfectly tender. Our tahini is stone-ground, our lemons squeezed fresh each morning, our olive oil imported directly from small family farms. No shortcuts. No compromises.

We believe a meal is a journey. Pull up a chair, tear a piece of pita, and let the food do the talking. You are always welcome at our table.
